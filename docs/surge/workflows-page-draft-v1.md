## SECTION 03 — WHAT WE ACTUALLY BUILD

**Eyebrow:** 03 / WORKFLOW BUILDS

**Headline:**
Five agent builds we deploy on day one.

**Each block: 2-line description, what it integrates with, the dollar or time outcome.**

### 01. INSURANCE CARRIER CORRESPONDENCE + SUPPLEMENT GAP DETECTION
The agent reads every Xactimate estimate that comes back from a carrier, compares it line-by-line against your scope and photo documentation, and flags the items the adjuster missed. Your office manager reviews and submits supplements with documentation in 90 seconds, not 90 minutes.
**Stack:** Xactimate, CompanyCam, ServiceTitan or JobNimbus
**Industry comparable:** Allianz reported an 80 percent reduction in claim processing time using agentic AI. Sedgwick's Sidekick Plus pilot processed 50,000 claim documents at over 98 percent accuracy.

### 02. LEAD INTAKE WITH 30-SECOND RESPONSE
A voice agent answers every inbound call 24/7, qualifies, captures details, books to your dispatch calendar, and texts the homeowner a confirmation. Your team gets a clean booked job, not a voicemail to chase later.
**Stack:** CallRail, ServiceTitan, your CRM
**Why it matters:** 78 percent of buyers choose the first contractor who calls them back. Top performers in the JobNimbus 2026 benchmark respond in under 30 minutes. Most contractors are taking 4 to 6 hours and losing the job before they ever quote it.

### 03. AR FOLLOW-UP THAT DOESN'T DEPEND ON YOUR OFFICE MANAGER REMEMBERING
Every invoice gets an automated cadence: 7-day check-in, 14-day reminder, 30-day escalation, 45-day call task created for a human. The agent reads payment status from your accounting system and stops the cadence the moment money lands.
**Stack:** QuickBooks Online, ServiceTitan, Stripe or whatever processor you use
**Why it matters:** Once an account goes 60 days past due, there's a 62 percent chance it happens again. Two-thirds of past-60 accounts hit 90 days. The compounding cost of a single missed reminder is real money. Sage research shows automated payment reminders move payment up by 5 days on average.

### 04. ESTIMATE DRAFTING FROM PHOTOS AND NOTES
Field tech walks the property with CompanyCam. The agent ingests the photos, your job notes, and supplier pricing, and produces a draft estimate in the format your office manager already sends. Office manager reviews, edits if needed, and sends in under two minutes.
**Stack:** CompanyCam, Hover, Xactimate, your accounting system
**Why it matters:** The estimating step is where most contractors lose 5 to 10 hours per estimator per week. Senior estimators bill out at $80 plus per hour loaded. The pure labor recovery on this one workflow is six figures per year for a mid-sized firm.

### 05. DAILY OPERATIONS BRIEF
Every Monday at 6 AM, the owner gets a one-screen email: open claims by stage, AR aging buckets, lead source performance for the week prior, dispatch utilization, supplements pending. No spreadsheets to pull. No status meeting required. The data lives in your existing systems. The agent assembles the picture.
**Stack:** Whatever you already use. We integrate, we don't replace.
**Why it matters:** Most owners are running blind on these numbers between monthly closes. The cost of running blind is decisions made on yesterday's gut feel, not this morning's facts.
