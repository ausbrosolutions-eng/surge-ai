# Reputation & Social Media

## Review Platform Priority
1. Google (73% of all reviews, impacts LSA and Map Pack)
2. Yelp (178M monthly visitors, critical for ChatGPT citations)
3. Angi/HomeAdvisor (trade-specific)
4. Facebook (social proof)
5. BBB (trust signal for AI entity authority)

## Review Generation System
1. Tech completes job, customer expresses satisfaction
2. Verbal ask: "Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review?"
3. SMS within 2 hours with direct Google review link
4. Email follow-up at 48 hours if no review
5. Second SMS at 5 days (maximum)

Target: 1-2 new Google reviews per week per location. Respond to 100%.

## Negative Review Response Framework
1. Acknowledge without admitting fault
2. Apologize for the experience
3. Take offline: "Please call [phone]"
4. Keep under 100 words
5. NEVER argue

## Social Platform Priority
1. Facebook (primary buyer is 35-65)
2. Nextdoor (most underutilized, neighborhood-verified)
3. Instagram (homeowners 25-40, Reels for discovery)
4. YouTube (long-term brand, dual SEO value)
5. TikTok (growing homeowner overlap)

## Post Frequency
- Facebook: 3-5x/week
- Instagram: 4-5x/week (Reels + static)
- Nextdoor: 2-3x/month (avoid over-promotion)
- YouTube: 1-2 videos/month
