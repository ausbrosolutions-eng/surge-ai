# Agency Operations

## Pricing Tiers

**Foundation ($1,000-$1,500/mo):** GBP setup/optimization, NAP audit, citation cleanup, review system, monthly posts, reporting

**Growth ($2,500-$4,000/mo):** + Full local SEO, blog content, reputation management, Google Ads management, bi-weekly calls

**Domination ($4,000-$8,000/mo + ad spend):** + LSA, social media (3 platforms), video content, landing page CRO, call tracking, weekly dashboards

## Client Onboarding (7-14 Days)
- Day 1: Contract + intake + access requests
- Day 2-3: Kickoff call (60-90 min)
- Day 3-4: Technical setup (CallRail, GA4, conversion tracking)
- Day 4-5: Baseline audit
- Day 8-9: Strategy presentation (30/60/90 plan)
- Day 10: Campaign launch
- Day 12-14: First deliverables live

**Key principle:** Deliver visible quick wins in first 30 days.

## Monthly Report Metrics
- Leads by channel, cost per lead by channel
- Phone calls with attribution
- GBP metrics (impressions, calls, directions)
- Local rankings (Local Falcon grid)
- Review count/rating/new reviews
- Website traffic and conversion rate
- Ad spend and ROAS
- Actions completed vs planned
- Next month plan

## Key KPIs

| KPI | Average | Top Performer |
|-----|---------|---------------|
| Cost Per Lead | $75-$200 | <$75 |
| Lead-to-Booked Rate | 30-50% | 60-80% |
| CLV:CAC Ratio | 3:1 min | 5:1+ |
| Website Conversion | 2-5% | 8-12% |
| Google Review Rating | 4.5 | 4.8+ |
| Call Answer Rate | 75% | 90%+ |
| LSA Booking Rate | 60% | 80%+ |
