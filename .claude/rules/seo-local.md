# Local SEO & GBP Optimization

## GBP Critical Rules
- Business name matches legal name exactly (NO keyword stuffing)
- Primary category = highest-revenue service
- All services listed with descriptions and pricing
- Minimum 10+ high-quality photos at launch; add 1-2 per month
- Post minimum 1-2 times per week (freshness is AI ranking signal)
- No posts or photos in 30+ days = significant ranking drop

## Citation Tiers

**Tier 1 (Build First):** Google Business Profile, Bing Places, Apple Maps, Yelp, BBB, Yellow Pages, Facebook Business, Foursquare, Nextdoor

**Tier 2 (Home Service):** Angi, HomeAdvisor, Houzz, Thumbtack, Porch, BuildZoom

**Tier 3 (Trade-Specific):** PHCC, NRCA, NALP, NECA, manufacturer dealer pages, Chamber of Commerce

**Data Aggregators:** Data Axle, Neustar Localeze, Foursquare, Factual

## Service Area Pages
- One unique page per major city/service area
- URL: `/plumber-dallas-tx/`, `/hvac-repair-fort-worth/`
- Unique content per page (NOT copy-paste with city name swapped)
- Include: local landmarks, neighborhoods, local reviews, embedded Google Map
- 500-800+ words per page; 1,000+ for competitive markets

## Schema Markup (JSON-LD)
- LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, HowTo, AggregateRating
- Validate with Google Rich Results Test; check Search Console monthly

## Rank Tracking Tools
- Local Falcon -- grid-based geo ranking (best for client reports)
- BrightLocal -- all-in-one for agencies
- Whitespark -- citation finder
