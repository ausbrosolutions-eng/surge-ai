# Google Ads & LSA Management

## LSA Ranking Factors (in order)
1. Review score -- maintain 4.8+ stars
2. Review quantity and recency
3. Response rate -- answer calls fast
4. Profile completeness
5. Booking rate -- aim for 80%+
6. Service area targeting -- tighter geo outperforms broad
7. GBP integration

## LSA Optimization
- Use Maximize Leads bidding (not Max Per Lead)
- Keep service area focused -- start tight, expand with budget
- Dispute invalid leads promptly
- Mark every lead as booked or not booked -- trains algorithm
- Connect GBP and LSA accounts to sync reviews

## Google Ads Campaign Structure
1. LSA Campaign (highest priority)
2. Branded Search (protect brand)
3. Service-Specific Search (separate per service)
4. Emergency/Urgent (highest-intent, higher bids)
5. Competitor Campaign (optional)
6. Remarketing Display

## Emergency Keywords (Convert 3-5x Higher)
Allocate 30-40% of budget to emergency terms.

## Smart Bidding Strategy
- First 30-60 days: Maximize Conversions
- After 30+ conversions/month: switch to Target CPA
- Set Target CPA at 150% of desired CPL initially, then tighten

## 2025 Benchmarks
- Avg CTR: 6.37%
- Avg CPC: $7.85 (electricians $12.18, painters $13.74, roofers $10.70)
- Target CPL: $50-$150 for most services
- Target ROAS: 3:1 minimum
